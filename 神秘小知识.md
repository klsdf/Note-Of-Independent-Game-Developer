
**AI slop** is [digital content](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_content "Digital content") made with [generative artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence "Generative artificial intelligence"), specifically when perceived to show a lack of effort, quality or deeper meaning, and an overwhelming volume of production.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_slop#cite_note-Hern-2024-1)[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_slop#cite_note-Read-2024-4)[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_slop#cite_note-NYT-2024-5) Coined in the 2020s, the term has a pejorative connotation similar to [spam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spamming "Spamming").[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_slop#cite_note-Read-2024-4)
